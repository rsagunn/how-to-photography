What is advertisment photograpy

Types of advertisment photography




Components of advertisment photography


Advertisement photography is a branch of commercial photography focused on creating visually appealing images that promote products, services, or brands. These images are used in marketing campaigns, online ads, billboards, magazines, and social media to attract customers.

  2. Food photography

  3. Real estate photography

  4. Event photography

  5. Corporate photography

  1. Camera settings

    Aperture (f/5.6 – f/11) for sharp images with good depth of field.
    Shutter Speed (1/60s – 1/200s) to avoid motion blur.
    ISO (100-400) depending on lighting conditions.










  2. Lighting Techniques

    Use soft, diffused light to reduce harsh shadows.
    A reflector or bounce card can fill in shadows and add highlights.
    A tripod helps stabilize the camera and maintain consistent framing.










  3. Composition Tips

    Use the rule of thirds to create balanced and visually appealing images.
    Include negative space to draw attention to the subject.
    Experiment with different angles and perspectives to find the best shot.
