What is manditory to know about photography?

  1. The exposure triangle

    ISO- Controls sensor sensitivity to light.

    Aperture- Controls the amount of light that enters the camera.

    Shutter speed- Controls the duration of light that enters the camera.











  2. Lighting and white balance

    Natural light is ideal; golden hour (sunrise/sunset) provides soft, warm tones.

    Adjust White Balance settings to match the lighting.










  3. Raw vs JPEG

    RAW – Higher quality, more editing flexibility, but larger file sizes.

    JPEG – Compressed, ready-to-use, but less detail for post-processing.










  4. Post-processing

    Enhance colors, sharpness, and exposure in editing software (Lightroom, Photoshop, etc.).
