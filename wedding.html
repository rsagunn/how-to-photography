Wedding photography




How to make your photos good

Wedding photography is a specialized type of photography that focuses on capturing the moments, emotions, and details of a wedding day. It combines elements of portrait, documentary, and event photography to tell a love story through pictures.



  1. Camera and lenses

    Full-frame DSLR or Mirrorless camera for high-quality images in various lighting.
    Prime Lenses (50mm, 85mm f/1.8 or f/1.4) for stunning portraits.
    Zoom Lenses (24-70mm, 70-200mm f/2.8) for flexibility.










  2. Lighting Techniques

    Use natural light for soft, romantic effects.
    A flash or speedlight helps in dark indoor receptions.
    Reflectors and diffusers improve harsh lighting conditions.










  3. Camera Settings

    Aperture (f/1.8 – f/4) for soft, dreamy backgrounds.
    Shutter Speed (1/200s – 1/500s) to capture moments sharply.
    ISO (100-3200) depending on lighting conditions.










  4. Post-Processing & Editing

    Adjust brightness, contrast, and colors.
    Skin retouching and removing distractions.
    Applying soft tones or black-and-white effects.
