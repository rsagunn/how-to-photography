High-Budget cameras

  1. Canon R100 w/ lens $800

    Experience the fun of RF mount content creation with the smallest and lightest EOS R series camera1, the Canon EOS R100. Excellent still-image capabilities start with its 24.1 Megapixel APS-C size CMOS Image sensor and DIGIC 8 processor, along with Dual Pixel CMOS AF with the ability to detect human faces and focus on their eyes. The EOS R100 also can record Full HD video at up to 60 frames per second, and detailed, high-res 4K from the central area of the sensor at 24 frames per second. Canon R100 w/ lens

  2. Used Sony a6000 $429

    The Sony A6000 is a competent APS-C mirrorless camera with a 24.3MP sensor, fast autofocus, and great-looking images in any light. It features continuous shooting, tilting LCD, and OLED electronic viewfinder. It is decent for travel photography due to its portability and good image quality. This is a camera that uses E-mount lenses if you are looking for lenses for this camera. Sony a6000

  1. Canon R50 ($970)

    Equipped with a 24.2-million-pixel CMOS (APS-C) image sensor and DIGIC X Image Processor, the EOS R50 camera can capture images and videos with reduced noise, delivering clear, detailed results even in low light. Images look beautiful even when enlarged, with natural bokeh that smartphones can't offer. Canon R50

  2. Sony a ZV-E10 ($900)

    The Sony ZV-E10 is a 24MP APS-C mirrorless interchangeable lens camera aimed at vloggers. It features an articulating selfie screen with touch capability, 4K video capture, headphone and microphone ports and a variety of add-on accessories to assist in video capture, including the handgrip seen in the photo above (sold separately) and a range of sophisticated microphones. Sony ZV-E10

  1. Sony a7IV ($3198)

    The Sony a7 IV is the fourth generation of the company's core a7 full-frame mirrorless camera model. It's the most advanced yet, bringing many of the improvements Sony has made in terms of autofocus and interface design since the launch of the a7 III, back in February 2018. A7IV

  2. Canon EOS R6 Mark II ($3200)

    The Canon EOS R6 II is a 24MP full-frame camera aimed at enthusiast photographers and video shooters. It may look identical to its predecessor on the outside, but it gains useful new features for stills and video while providing a refined shooting experience. Canon EOS R6 Mark II
