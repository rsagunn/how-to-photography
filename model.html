  1. Camera settings

    Aperture (f/1.8 – f/4) for soft, dreamy backgrounds.
    Shutter Speed (1/200s – 1/500s) to capture moments sharply.
    ISO (100-400) depending on lighting conditions.










  2. Lighting Techniques

    Use natural light for soft, romantic effects.
    A flash or speedlight helps in dark indoor receptions.
    Reflectors and diffusers improve harsh lighting conditions.










  3. Background & Composition

    Keep backgrounds simple to emphasize the model.
    Use the Rule of Thirds for balanced composition.
    Add props & styling to enhance the theme.










  4. Post-Processing and Editing

    Skin retouching for a polished look.
    Adjust colors, contrast, and lighting for a professional finish.
