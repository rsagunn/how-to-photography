Camera Bodies



Lenses



Accessories



There is many different camera bodies that you can choose from. The most popular ones are Canon, Nikon, and Sony.
2 of the main cameras are mirrorless and DSLR. Mirrorless cameras are smaller and lighter than DSLR cameras. They are also quieter and have a faster autofocus. DSLR cameras are bigger and heavier than mirrorless cameras. They have a longer battery life and a wider selection of lenses than a DSLR.

A lens is a tool used to bring light to a fixed focal point. In a film camera, the lens sends the light to the film strip, while in a digital camera (like DSLRs or mirrorless cameras), the lens directs light to a digital sensor. You wouldnt need all of the accessories if you are a beginner but these are really helpful tools for your photography.
