What is a flash

Recommended flashes

Camera flashes are devices used to produce a burst of light, typically used to light up a scene when taking a photograph.

  1. Godox V1

    The Godox V1 is a versatile and powerful flash with a round head design that provides soft even lighting. It features a rechargeable battery, TTL compatibility, and high-speed sync, making it suitable for photography needs.

  2. Godox V860 II

    The Godox V860 II is a powerful and versatile flash that offers TTL compatibility, high-speed sync, and a rechargeable battery. It features a built-in radio transmitter, making it easy to control multiple flashes wirelessly.

  3. Godox AD200 Pro

    The Godox AD200 Pro is a powerful and versatile flash that offers 200Ws of power, TTL compatibility, and high-speed sync. It features a built-in radio transmitter, a rechargeable battery, and a modular design that allows you to use different heads for different lighting effects.
