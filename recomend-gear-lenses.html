  1. Canon RF 50mm f/1.8 STM($270) & Canon RF 24-105mm f/4-7.1 IS STM($550)

    Canon RF 50mm f/1.8 STM The Canon RF 50mm f/1.8 STM is a compact, lightweight, and affordable prime lens. It's often referred to as the "nifty fifty" due to its focal length and wide f/1.8 aperture, which makes it great for portraits, low-light photography, and everyday use. The lens offers impressive image quality, with sharpness and good bokeh. It's also very portable, making it a great addition to any photographer's kit.
    Canon RF 24-105mm f/4-7.1 IS STM The Canon RF 24-105mm f/4-7.1 IS STM is a zoom lens with a broad focal range, making it suitable for various types of photography, from landscapes to portraits. It features image stabilization, which helps reduce camera shake, and a macro mode for close-up shots. While the variable aperture (f/4-7.1) limits its performance in low light, the lens is lightweight and affordable, making it a good option for everyday use. Both lenses offer good performance for their price points and are great additions to the Canon RF lineup.
