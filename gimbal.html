Whats a gimbal

Recomended gimbals

A gimbal is a device that allows an object to rotate smoothly on one or more axis. Its used in photography and videography to stabilize cameras and keep them level, even when the person holding the camera is moving. This makes your shots steady, without thinking how much you're moving. Gimbals come in various forms, including handheld models for smartphones and larger ones for professional cameras. They can have 2-axis or 3-axis stabilization, providing smoother and more comprehensive stabilization.
